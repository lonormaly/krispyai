## First comment (from the maker) — post immediately at launch

> Hey Product Hunt 👋 I'm [name], and this is Krispy.
>
> Here's the problem I built it for: I want to talk to visitors on my landing pages, but every
> option is a bad trade. A dumb bot that answers nothing, or a $100–400/mo SaaS to get a real
> human in the loop — priced per seat, with a login my visitor never asked for, and all our
> chats on someone else's servers. I'm one person. That math never works.
>
> **Krispy is the third option:** an AI answers the easy stuff in *your* voice, and the moment a
> real person is needed, it pings **you on Telegram**. You reply from your phone and it lands
> live in the visitor's chat. Your phone is the dashboard — no new inbox to babysit.
>
> - 🥐 **Open source (MIT)** — read it, fork it, own it
> - 🥐 **Free to self-host** — one deploy on Cloudflare's free tier, no API key, no per-seat tax

> - 🥐 **Human handoff, built in** — the AI is the first touch, never the last word
>
> There's also **Krispy Cloud** ($19/mo flat, 14-day trial) if you'd rather not deploy anything —
> but self-host is first-class and always free.
>
> I'm here all day and I'd love your honest take — especially where it breaks or what channel you'd
> want after Telegram. And if you fork it or ship it somewhere, tell me, that genuinely makes my

## Launch-day engagement plan

- **00:01 PT** — live. Maker's first comment posted immediately.
- **Notify your people honestly** — the founding members, Discord, newsletter: *"Krispy's live on
  PH today. If you like it, an upvote and an honest comment mean a lot. No script, no pressure."*
  (Never buy votes or use engagement pods — PH penalizes it and this crowd can smell it.)
- **Answer every comment within minutes**, all day. Warm, specific, Buttr-flavored in the chrome,
  clear on the substance. Ask commenters what channel they'd want next — turns a comment into a
  conversation and a roadmap signal.
- **Cross-pollinate** — the X thread and LinkedIn post link to the PH page in the morning so your
  own audience can pile in early (early velocity matters most).
- **Convert the spike** — every reply nudges toward Discord or the newsletter. PH traffic
  evaporates by tomorrow; an owned relationship doesn't.
- **End of day** — post the result + a thank-you in Discord and on X. Screenshot the ranking for
  the build-in-public archive whether it's #1 or #12. Honesty is the brand.
