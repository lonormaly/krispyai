## Notes for the founder

- Swap `[X weeks]` and the per-seat story for what's true. Specific + honest beats punchy + vague.
- Reply to your own thread through the day with the PH milestones, a bug you fixed live, a nice
  comment someone left. Keep it alive; the algorithm rewards a thread that keeps moving.
- When people reply "does it do Slack/WhatsApp?" — answer every one, ask what they'd want next.
  That's your roadmap and your community, forming in public.
- Don't manufacture a tagline closer on every tweet. End when the point's made (brand-voice rule 6).
